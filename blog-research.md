### 4. Understanding Gold Purity (Education)
- 24K = 99.9% pure, softest
- 22K = 91.67% pure, good for jewelry
- 18K = 75% pure, most durable for jewelry
- Gold bars: sold by weight, purity stamped
- Gold coins: collectible premium over bars
- Gold jewelry: price exceeds gold content due to craftsmanship

### 5. How to Invest in Physical Gold (Guide)
- Gold bars/bullion: sold by weight
- Gold coins: American Eagle, Krugerrand, Maple Leaf
- Gold jewelry: 18K, 22K, 24K
- Start small: 1-gram or 1/10-ounce bars
- Experts recommend 5-15% of net worth in gold
- Spot price vs futures price explained
